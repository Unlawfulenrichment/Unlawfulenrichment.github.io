    • 山东太谷县农民律师周广立十年的行政诉讼经历

      • 作者注意到了周的【律师经历阶段】，阶段划分参见应星：《“迎法入乡”与“接近正义”————对中国乡村“赤脚律师”的个案研究》
        • 初起阶段：主要涉及本地乡政府，逐渐获得农民认可，与本地政府关系紧张。
        • 争议阶段：周广立现象得到媒体关系，影响逐渐扩大上升。
          • 阳谷县政府批判周广立现象。
          • 2000年新华社关于周广立现象的上内参。省市委主要领导批示。同年市县组成调查组，对周广立现象进行了深入调查。调查组最后的结论是：“周广立现象 ”对社会有利,受到了群众的信任和欢迎；同时,对这一现象要加以正确对待和引导,并加以规范。
          • Q：不同层级政府的态度？以及政府系统对于——信访——vs——行政诉讼的不同态度，这种态度是理性的吗？（出于政治绩效、考核以及压力的不同？还是有一定的观念影响，上访意味着敌对而诉讼不是？）
        • 相对自主阶段：市县联合调查组的调查报告含糊地提出了要引导 “周广立现象 ”,但并没有说如何引导。由于调查报告的基调是大体肯定的,所以,县乡政府从此不再敢对周广立施加压力了。
          • Q：为什么没有后续引导？为什么县乡政府之后又开始阻碍了？（因为内参作用耗散？省委领导的离开？县乡主政者的调动？周广立影响的不断升级？代理了一些格外敏感触及红线的案子？）
        • 收缩阶段：应星认为，随着时间的流逝,市县联合调查报告给他定性的 “保护伞 ”作用也开始消褪。——为什么会耗散？

调解律师

  • 张永华. “诉源治理”视角下律师权威的塑造[J]. 河北法学, 2023, 41 (05): 135-153.
    • 本文问题：诉源治理中律师通过调解基层社会纠纷参与基础社会治理，Q：律师调解的权威是如何获得的？它的行使又存在哪些障碍？
      • 【时代背景】中国社会格局的转型，导致了人民调解的衰落，人们日益诉诸正式纠纷解决途径。
      • 【制度背景】多元纠纷解决机制建设
      • 【定义】律师调解是指律师、依法成立的律师调解工作室或者律师调解中心作为中立第三方主持调解，协助纠纷各方当事人通过自愿协商达成协议解决争议的活动。
      • 【领域】各类民商事纠纷，以及刑事诉讼附带民事诉讼的民事部分——不包括行政诉讼
      • 【场所】人民法院、公共服务中心、律师协会以及律师事务所设立
      • 【费用】
        • 律师事务所-有偿和低价的原则向双方当事人收取调解费
        • 公共法律服务中心（站）、律师协会设立的律师调解中心，由司法行政机关、律师协会通过政府采购服务的方式解决经费。律师调解员调解法律援助案件的经费，由法律援助机构通过政府采购服务渠道予以解决。
        • 人民法院设立律师调解工作室的，由政府采购服务渠道解决调解经费，并纳入人民法院专项预算。
    • 律师和“诉源治理”为什么有关系？律师为何在现实承担起了“诉源治理”的任务？

authoritarian legality

these authoritarian states have a legal system with duality——“law matters
sometimes, but not always”. The courts and other state organs are still pursuing the public good when it will not weaken political control.

  • 【main debate】whether the dual legal system will bolster or undermine the political stability?
  • 【privious reseach】usually focus on the political control over the courts not the lawyers.

legal profession research in authoritarian country

  • Stern, Rachel E., and Lawrence J. Liu. “The Good Lawyer: State-Led Professional Socialization in Contemporary China.” Law & Social Inquiry 45.1 (2020): 226–248. Web.

    • how authoritarian states like China define and communicate notions of appropriate work conduct and professional excellence to lawyers.
    • two organization have the direct responsibility for overseeing
      the bar: the ministry of justice and the all china lawyer association.
    • three main channels to signal the state's expectations to the legal professions——
      • bar exam: the politicization of the Chinese bar exam
      • administrative rules《律师执业管理办法》、《律师事务所管理办法》
        • only courteous, by-the-book courtroom advocacy is permitted.
        • the newly revised Administrative Measure requires to establich the part committees in law firms.
      • state-sanctioned national outstanding lawyer award.
        • male lawyers with close ties to the government: working advising the government, providing individual legal aid, and bridging the government and the governed.
        • certain types consistently wins the award: : a well-educated man working in private practice who holds a leadership position in the ACLA or his local bar association.
    • 【research method】interviews, government documents, and a database of bar exam questions and award recipients.
      • diachronic method: analyze the evolution of the bar exam and the changes of the regulate rules.
      • 【arenasof professionalism】 key sites where the Chinese authorities detail their vision of the proper role of lawyers in society.
      • 【legal professionalism】not an unitary set of values, but consists of multiple visions of what constitutes proper behavior by lawyers.
        • refrence: Nelson, Robert L., and David M. Trubek. “Arenas of Professionalism: The Professional Ideologies of Lawyers in Context.” In Lawyers’ Ideals/Lawyers’ Practices: Transformations in the American Legal
          Profession. Edited by Robert L. Nelson, David M. Trubek, and Rayman L. Solomon, 177–214. Ithaca, NY: Cornell University Press, 1992.
      • 【legal activism under authoritarianism】“依法抗争”
      • 【the state's ideology of a good lawer】includes a positive,affirmative vision of political participation. Lawyers are encouraged
        to be partners in governance by serving as government advisors, providing public goods through legal aid, and easing communication between officials and the public.
      • the altitude of the outstanding lawyers: express negative altitude towards the lawyer activists. the elite lawyers believe that the legal reform is a step-by-step process.——【two key elements】optimistic and patience.
      • criminal defense lawyers: more working experience is colleated with political liberal altitude.(Sida Liu and Terence Halliday)
      • 【Background】the emergence of a private bar turn the lawyer from civil servants to legal activists. And the the ministry of justive and local justice bureau tried to tighten the political control.
      • 【 research innovation】political control also includes professional socialization. Except the coersion and detterence, the state also use channels to signal the state's expectations to the legal professions.
